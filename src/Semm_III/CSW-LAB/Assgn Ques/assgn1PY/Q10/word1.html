Defenestration

Defenestration is a term used to describe the act of throwing someone out of a window.

Uses of the Word

  • The defenestration of the diplomat was an international incident.
  • During the riot, several were injured due to the defenestration of office furniture.
  • The historical event known as the Defenestration of Prague marked a significant moment in European history.

Synonyms and Antonyms

Type Words
Synonyms ejection, expulsion
Antonyms entry, inclusion
